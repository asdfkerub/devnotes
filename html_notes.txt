HTML NOTES


AUDIO
<audio controls loop autoplay>
    <source src="desperado.ogg">
    <source src="desperado.mp3">
</audio>

To play audio in different browsers. controls loop autoplay can be disabled by removing.
Firefox - .ogg
Safari - .mp3
Chrome - .ogg & .mp3


VIDEO
<video width="400" height="200" poster="picture.jpg" controls>
    <source src="caddyshack.ogv">
    <source src="movie.mp4">
</video>

To play video in different browsers.
Firefox - .ogv
Safari - .mp4
Chrome - .ogv & .mp4


AUTOFOCUS
<label for="food">What is your favorite food?</label>
<input id="food" name="food" type="text" autofocus>

Automatically focuses on the input once the document is loaded.
